Chapter 2:  Vendor Registration and Sign Up
    - First of all, you need to register as a vendor and submit your documents for verification purposes, in the scenario you are an upcoming enterprise or entrepreneur without the needed documentation, you would need to upload the following documents for verification purposes: 
        1.	Your National Id
        2.	A Recent Picture not older than 3 months
        3.	Provide you with the location and goods you will be selling for documentation purposes.
        4.	You would need a Guarantor (A verified Vendor who has been on Itelix for more than 6 months)
        5.	An account you want payments to be made to and paid out from.
        6.	Your Tax documentation is need, if not too, we would help you apply for it.
        7.	You can then customize your store; accordingly, you can make changes later which would be submitted for a review and take 3 to 7 business days to be reviewed.
        8.	After your vendor profile creation note that all sales you make would be paid to you in these options: Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly or Quarterly.
